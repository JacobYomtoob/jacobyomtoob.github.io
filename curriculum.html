Original Programs

Curricula I Created

These programs were built from the ground up to address gaps in resident neurology education.

2025 – Present · Northwestern · Chicago, IL

Resident Movement Phenomenology Rounds

Designed and launched quarterly video-based movement phenomenology sessions for neurology residents. The curriculum uses curated, annotated clinical video cases to build visual pattern recognition and diagnostic reasoning in movement disorders — a skill rarely developed through traditional lecture formats.


Quarterly Sessions Video-Based Resident Education
2024 – Present · Northwestern · Chicago, IL

HD Care Partner Bootcamp Series

Developing and instituting a structured Huntington's Disease care partner education program. Collected focus group preferences to shape the bootcamp curriculum and is measuring impact on caregiver burden through validated outcome tools. Mentor: Dr. Danny Bega, MD, MSCI.


Patient & Family Education Curriculum Research Outcomes-Driven
2023 – 2024 · Mass General Brigham · Boston, MA

Movement After Dark

Founded quarterly movement disorders phenomenology video rounds for neurology residents and fellows with movement faculty Dr. Albert Hung. This resident-driven curriculum served as the predecessor model for the current Northwestern program and was presented at the Scholarship in Neurology Education Symposium (2024).


Resident-Driven Presented at SNE 2024
2022 – 2023 · Mass General Brigham · Boston, MA

RITE Review Email Series

Designed and delivered a residency-wide daily email curriculum providing curated RITE board preparation questions with detailed, teaching-oriented answer explanations to support structured knowledge consolidation in the lead-up to the board exam.
