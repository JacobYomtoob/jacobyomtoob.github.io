About

Jacob Yomtoob, MD

I am a Movement Disorders Fellow at Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine, where I am also participating in the McGaw Health Professions Educator Clinical Scholars Program.

My clinical and research focus is movement disorders — including Parkinson's disease, Huntington's disease, ataxia, and hyperkinetic movement disorders. My educational work centers on resident and fellow training in movement disorders phenomenology, curriculum development, and patient and caregiver education.

I welcome inquiries related to research collaboration, speaking invitations, educational program development, and mentorship.
